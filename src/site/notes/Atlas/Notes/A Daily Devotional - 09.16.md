Matthew ends his Gospel with the victory of Jesus. Early on the first day of the week, Mary Magdalene and the other Mary go to the tomb. They find an angel waiting for them. His appearance is so bright and powerful that it frightens them. The angel tells them the good news: Jesus has risen. As they hurry to tell the disciples, Jesus himself appears. They have all the evidence of a miracle, but they still refuse to believe. They even invent a lie to fight against the truth. Some people will not believe, even when the risen Lord stands before them. That was true then, and it is still true today.
