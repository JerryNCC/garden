### 1. **popular (adj)**

**受欢迎的**  
这种手机在年轻人中非常**受欢迎**。  
这个歌手在你的国家**受欢迎**吗？

> [!info]- **In English**
> - This phone is very **popular** among young people.
> - Is this singer **popular** in your country?

### 2. **ski (v)**

> - We like to go **skiing** in the mountains during winter.
> - Can you **ski**?
