### 1. **anxious (adj)**

**焦虑的**  
她对考试结果感到很**焦虑**。  
你为什么看起来这么**焦虑**？

> [!info]- **English**
> - She feels **anxious** about her exam results.
> - Why do you look so **anxious**?

### 2. **awful (adj)**

**糟糕的**

> [!info]- **English**
> - The food at that restaurant was **awful**.
> - Do you think this movie is **awful**?
