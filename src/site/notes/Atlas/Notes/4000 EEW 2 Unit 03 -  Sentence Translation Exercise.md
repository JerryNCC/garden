### 1. **arrow (n)**

**箭**  
他用弓射出了一支**箭**。  
你能射中目标的**箭**吗？

> [!info]- **English**
> - He shot an **arrow** with his bow.
> - Can you hit the target with an **arrow**?

**战斗**

> [!info]- **English**
> - It was a tough **battle**.
> - Who won the **battle**?
